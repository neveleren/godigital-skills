## AIDA Color Strategy

- **Attention:** #4ed892 (Green) - grab attention with vibrant, positive color
- **Interest:** #b5ddc5 (Light Green) - soft, calming for benefits
- **Desire:** #132136 (Dark Blue) - professional, trustworthy
- **Action:** #4ed892 (Green) - motivate action with positivity
