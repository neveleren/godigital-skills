## Autotrader Listing Structure

### 1. Title (Max 70 characters)

**Format:** `[Condition] [Key Feature] [Model] - [Highlight]`

**Examples:**
- `Immaculate Full-Spec SL43 AMG - MANUFAKTUR Alpine Grey`
- `Near-New Mercedes-AMG SL43 - Every Option, 3,000km`
- `Rare MANUFAKTUR SL43 AMG - Full Spec, As New`

**Rules:**
- Lead with condition or rarity
- Include model name
- Add one compelling differentiator
- Avoid ALL CAPS except model names

---

## Writing Guidelines

### DO:
- Use aspirational language ("exceptional," "exclusive," "pinnacle")
- Emphasize rarity and exclusivity
- Highlight condition and low mileage
- List features systematically
- Include warranty and service history
- Provide clear contact method
- Use professional tone throughout

### DON'T:
- Use desperate language ("must sell," "price negotiable")
- Overuse capitals or exclamation marks
- Include negative information in listing
- Compare to cheaper alternatives
- Use excessive jargon
- Make unverifiable claims
- Include personal financial circumstances

---

## Pricing Guidance

For ICP buyers, price positioning matters:

| Position | Strategy |
|----------|----------|
| **At Market** | Emphasize full spec, condition, included extras |
| **Above Market** | Justify with rarity, MANUFAKTUR, unique options |
| **Below Market** | Position as exceptional value, motivated seller |

**Never mention:**
- "Negotiable" in headline
- Desperation or urgency
- Financial difficulties

## Photo Guidelines

**Required shots:**
1. Front 3/4 view (hero shot)
2. Rear 3/4 view
3. Side profile (both sides if possible)
4. Front view
5. Rear view
6. Interior dashboard
7. Interior seats
8. Engine bay (if presentable)
9. Wheel detail
10. Any unique features (colored calipers, special trim)

**Quality standards:**
- Clean vehicle
- Good lighting (golden hour ideal)
- Uncluttered background
- High resolution
- No watermarks from other dealers
