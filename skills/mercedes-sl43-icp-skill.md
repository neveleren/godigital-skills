#### Occupation

- CEO / Managing Director
- Business Owner / Entrepreneur
- Senior Executive (C-Suite)
- Medical Specialist (Surgeon, etc.)

| Attribute | Description |

## Pain Points & Desires

### What They Want

1. **Status & Recognition** - A car that reflects their success
2. **Exclusivity** - Not seeing their car everywhere
3. **Quality & Reliability** - Mercedes-Benz brand trust
4. **Performance** - Thrilling driving experience
5. **Convertible Lifestyle** - Enjoying Cape Town's weather
6. **Modern Technology** - Latest infotainment, safety features
7. **Comfort** - Grand touring capability for longer drives
8. **Investment Value** - Low depreciation for near-new vehicle

### What They Fear

1. Buying a car with hidden problems
2. Overpaying compared to market value
3. Dealing with unprofessional sellers
4. Missing out on a rare, well-specced vehicle
5. Depreciation (mitigated by low mileage)

---

## Purchase Triggers

1. **Business Success** - Closed a big deal, bonus, profit distribution
2. **Milestone** - Birthday (50th, 60th), anniversary, retirement
3. **Upgrade** - Current car is aging, wants something special
4. **Lifestyle Change** - Kids left home, more disposable income
5. **Opportunity** - Found the perfect spec at the right price
6. **Tax Planning** - Year-end vehicle purchase for business

## Messaging Strategy

### What Resonates

| Message Type | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| **Exclusivity** | "Rare MANUFAKTUR specification" |
| **Full Spec** | "Every optional extra fitted" |
| **Condition** | "Near-new, only 3,000 km" |
| **Value** | "Significant saving vs new" |
| **Heritage** | "The iconic SL, reimagined" |
| **Performance** | "AMG engineering excellence" |
| **Lifestyle** | "Open-top grand touring" |

### What to Avoid

- Desperation ("Must sell!")
- Excessive price negotiation language
- Technical jargon overload
- Comparison to "cheaper" alternatives
- Any suggestion of problems or issues

## Decision Logic

When creating content for Mercedes-AMG SL43 sales:

```
IF target_audience = "ICP Buyer"
THEN emphasize:
  - Exclusivity and rarity
  - Full specification details
  - Impeccable condition
  - Professional presentation
  - Lifestyle benefits
  - Brand heritage
  
AVOID:
  - Price-focused messaging
  - Urgency tactics
  - Comparisons to lesser vehicles
  - Any negativity or desperation
```

## Output Format for Listings

When using this ICP to create listings:

1. **Headline**: Emphasize key differentiators (spec, condition, exclusivity)
2. **Opening**: Appeal to lifestyle and aspiration
3. **Features**: Organized by category, highlight premium options
4. **Closing**: Professional call-to-action, easy contact method
